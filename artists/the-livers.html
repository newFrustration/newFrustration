Long time friends and frequent collaborators Tyler Schmalenbach, Nathan Petersen, and Jacob Tressel formed The Livers in the summer of 2018, hoping to create something in line with conversations they had held about the importance of synchronicity between visual and auditory art. The experience would turn out to be a test of momentum.

With an improvised jam as the base, the trio performed solos, sampled, arranged, and mastered their track in the same day that they filmed and edited the accompanying video.

New Frustration is proud to present The Livers’ music video "The Livers,” also available in the form of isolated audio, and perfectly poised for a fit of nostalgia.
