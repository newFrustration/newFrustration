Nathan Petersen

Soundcloud

Nathan Petersen is probably not the only saxophonist with Kendrick Lamar’s “DAMN” hung on his wall. But he’s the only one that you should be paying attention to right now.

In addition to his work with Crunchy Love and The Livers, the 21 year old axeman is already a go-to feature artist in the East Bay, for recording sessions as well as live performances. He’s held seats in prestigious groups at the California Jazz Conservatory, featured on braggadocious trap records, and sat in with basement bands all across Berkeley. Now, he’s taking the lead.
