MASINO doesn’t remember what sample he used on your favorite track. A visit to his studio will reveal any number of vinyls strewn across the couch or stacked haphazardly next to a synth. The mystery is part of the fun for him. He may spend hours dropping the needle on records searching for the perfect pocket, weeks constructing the sonics surrounding it, and countless nights getting the master right. But he doesn’t cling to his discoveries like prizes, he’s on to the next beat.
