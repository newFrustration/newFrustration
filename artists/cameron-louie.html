Cameron Louie

Instagram

"I'd rather work than fuck. I'd rather work than sleep."
-Cameron Louie, Dec 31 2018

And it shows. The Berkeley based powerhouse does just about everything, and it's not quite enough for him yet. He’s got microfractures in his right hand from drawing, painting, and drumming almost every waking hour. In addition to New Frustration signees Earthworm, he plays in no less than two bands at all times. Odds are, if you buy him a drink or two, he’ll sit in on your project. And he’s recently gotten into directing.

New Frustration is proud to present Cameron's directorial debut, "distracted."
