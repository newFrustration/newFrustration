New Frustration is proud to present MASINO’s debut project, “Recorded Thoughts.” While holding true to his earlier soul-based work, it finds MASINO stretching out into less familiar territory. More diverse samples, non-traditional song structures, and live instrumentation give way to a display of power that showcases the versatility of the producer while defining his sound and paying homage to early influences such as MF DOOM, 9th wonder, and J Dilla.
